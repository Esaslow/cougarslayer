Returning 2025

After years of underground legend status, The Cougar Slayer returns! What started in 2012 as a self-timed Strava event has grown into Boulder County's most notorious mountain bike challenge.

"The course of the eponymous underground, unsanctioned, unofficial, mountain bike race/adventure."

This epic journey takes you from the mountains to plains on Boulder County's best and most obscure mountain bike byways, combining technical singletrack, challenging climbs, and breathtaking descents.
